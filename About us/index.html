About Us

Founded in 2015 by Dr. B.K. Mishra, Veterans India is a national, apolitical, people-powered patriotic movement with over 3.8 million members.

Our inclusive philosophy—"Anyone who can say Jai Hind and Vande Mataram can join Veterans India"—unites citizens across all divides. We foster nationalism, empower youth, support veterans and martyrs' families, and contribute to a self-reliant and progressive Bharat.

Our Initiatives

Patriotic Events

Vijay Diwas celebrations, patriotic runs on Republic & Independence Day, uniting citizens in national pride.

Awards & Welfare

Pride of Nation Awards and programs to support martyrs' families and veterans.

Youth & Empowerment

Self-defense for women, rural sports meets, skill-building & educational opportunities.

Education & Collaborations

Partnered with AICTE, JNU, BYJU's, and more to deliver learning and nation-building programs.

Environment & Cleanliness

Swachh Bharat drives, tree planting, water conservation for a cleaner, greener India.
